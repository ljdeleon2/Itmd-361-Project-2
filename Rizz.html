Why? Billionaire, genius, playboy, and philanthropist. - Effortlessly smooth, whether in a suit or the Iron Man armor. - Even Pepper Potts, who rejected him countless times, couldn’t resist in the end.

Why? The most awkwardly smooth guy ever. - Pulled **MJ, Gwen Stacy, AND Black Cat** despite being broke and awkward. - His mix of **humor, confidence, and kindness** makes him **undeniable rizz material**.

3. Star-Lord (Peter Quill)

Why? Galaxy-wide rizz. - Even **Gamora, the most serious warrior in the galaxy, fell for him**. - His **80s music, humor, and confidence** make him **impossible to ignore**.

4. Wolverine (Logan)

Why? The ultimate bad-boy energy. - Despite his gruff nature, he’s had **Jean Grey, Mystique, and even Storm in his life**. - His **brooding attitude + mysterious past = peak rizz energy**.

5. Thor

Why? A literal god and warrior. - Pulled **Jane Foster, Sif, and countless admirers across realms**. - His **confidence, battle skills, and legendary status** make him an **irresistible force**.
